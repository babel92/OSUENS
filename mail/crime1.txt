Dear Ohio State Community Member:

You are receiving this notice because a crime has occurred that is considered to be of a concern and/or a continuing threat to the campus community.

The purpose of this message is to heighten your safety awareness by providing community members with information necessary to take appropriate precautions, enable you to take actions to help increase your safety, and to aid in the prevention of similar crimes.

This notice does not mean there has been an increase in crime or that any specific crime pattern exists.

On 10/14/17, at approximately 12:12 p.m., an unknown male entered the Thompson Library and appeared to be following a female student. The female student became uncomfortable by his presence and left the area. The man then left the building. Police believe the unknown male is the same individual reported to have previously committed public indecency in the library.

Information is being shared so community members can take precautions and be alert. A photo of the suspect is below. He is described as a black male, between the ages of 25 and 35, with a black goatee and mustache. Reports indicate he was last seen wearing a white baseball hat, white rimmed sunglasses, a gray t-shirt, black pants and black tennis shoes with white soles.
